The Mercedes-Benz G-class was redesigned in 2019, but for 2023 the SUV sees only a few minor equipment changes.
New for 2023, a G Professional package includes a roof rack, spare wheel holder, cherry wood trim for the luggage compartment,
and matte black 18-inch wheels. An optional logo package is also newly available.

  1. Turbocharged 4.0-liter V8 engine
    (416 horsepower, 450 lb-ft of torque)
  2. Nine-speed automatic transmission.
  3. Two-speed transfer case with low-range gearing.
  4. 19-inch wheels.
